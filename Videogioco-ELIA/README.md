# NeonBreak: Cyberpunk Arkanoid

A retro-style Arkanoid game with a cyberpunk theme. Challenge your dad to beat your high score in this multi-level brick breaker game!

## Features

- Cyberpunk visual style with neon colors and grid effects
- 5 levels with increasing difficulty
- Power-ups system (extended paddle, multi-ball, etc.)
- High score tracking with localStorage
- Responsive controls

## How to Play

1. Open the `src/index.html` file in your browser
2. Use the left and right arrow keys to move the paddle
3. Press the spacebar to launch the ball
4. Break all the bricks to complete each level
5. Try to achieve the highest score!

## Game Controls

- **Move Paddle**: Left and Right Arrow Keys
- **Launch Ball**: Space
- **Pause Game**: ESC

## Power-ups

- **+**: Extends your paddle
- **-**: Shrinks your paddle
- **⊿**: Slows down the ball
- **⋀**: Speeds up the ball
- **◎**: Creates multiple balls
- **♥**: Gives an extra life

## Tips

- Different brick types require multiple hits to break
- Some bricks are indestructible (glowing yellow)
- Try to catch power-ups for advantages
- Complete levels quickly for time bonuses

Enjoy the game and good luck!
